# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 21, 2026

## Introduction

This application ("App") is owned and operated by SaleSwift.

The App is intended solely for internal business use by authorized personnel and is not available to the general public. The App integrates with Meta platforms, including Facebook and Instagram, to analyze advertising data and provide AI-powered insights and recommendations.

---

## Information We Access

When authorized by a user, the App may access information from Meta platforms, including:

* Advertising account information
* Campaign, ad set, and advertisement data
* Advertising performance metrics
* Business account information
* Facebook Pages and Instagram account information
* Other information explicitly authorized through Meta permissions

The App only accesses data required to perform its intended business functions.

---

## Purpose of Data Processing

The information accessed by the App is used exclusively for:

* Advertising analysis
* Campaign performance monitoring
* AI-generated recommendations and insights
* Internal reporting
* Business operations related to digital advertising

Data is not used for marketing to third parties or sold to external organizations.

---

## AI Services

Advertising and business data may be processed using artificial intelligence technologies to generate:

* Performance summaries
* Optimization recommendations
* Campaign insights
* Operational reports

The information is processed solely to support internal business activities.

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade any information obtained through the App.

Information may only be shared with:

* Authorized employees and contractors of SaleSwift
* Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers necessary to operate the App
* Service providers supporting AI functionality
* Legal authorities when required by applicable law

---

## Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Access to the App and its data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

---

## Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary for operational, business, legal, and security purposes.

Data may be deleted when it is no longer required.

---

## Meta Platform Compliance

This App operates in accordance with Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies.

All data accessed through Meta APIs is used solely for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.
